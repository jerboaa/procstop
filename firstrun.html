ProcSTOP Getting Started Guide

What is ProcSTOP?

ProcSTOP is an extension for Google Chrome which tries to help you with getting better at managing your time which you spend casually browsing the Web.

How does ProcSTOP work?

Browsing Time Awareness Training

The idea of ProcSTOP is to playfully train you to get better at estimating the time you spend online. By doing so it aims to increase awareness of the actual time a user spends online. We try to achieve this by allowing users to regain control over how they want to use their spare time and perhaps their productive hours as well.

ProcSTOP sets up a timer which expires randomly within a given range. Once the timer expires the user is notified by a pop-up notification and a flashing add-on icon.

Taking a Break from Browsing by Doing a Stretch

Then, the user has the possibility to see their actual online time. But before users get to see that they are encouraged to take a step back, relax, get their thoughts off what they were currently doing by showing a fitness video with instructions as to how to stretch their back. The idea of this is to get the sole attention of the user for a couple of minutes.

Guessing Game

Next, the user will be asked to guess their time they think they have spent online since they clicked the "Start" button of the add-on. In the next step the actual guessing time is shown and the actual time spent online on that day is recorded.

Satisfaction with Actual Time Spent Browsing

In the final step the user is asked for their level of satisfaction with the time they actually spent online. The add-on keeps a history of this measure of satisfaction together with the time spent online.
